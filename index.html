Intro

One-Hundred Days

of Rap Lyrics

Inspiration

Elle Luna, 100 Day Project

Fonts

Google Web Fonts

This is a collision of a couple of my favorite things, typography and rap music. After being inspired by Elle Luna’s #The100DayProject, I decided to embark on my own 100 day project — typesetting my favorite rap lyrics. One lyric a day, each day, for 100 days.
